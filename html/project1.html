ICT Posting Portal

Portal Overview

The ICT Posting Portal is a centralized platform where industry partners can connect with students and staff from the School of Information and Communication Technology. It serves as a hub for internships, student projects, and job placements, streamlining the process of matching students with potential employers.



Industry Collaboration

By fostering collaboration between academia and industry, the portal ensures that students receive relevant and practical knowledge. This prepares them for real-world challenges and aligns their skills with the needs of future employers.



Streamlining Recruitment

The ICT portal simplifies the recruitment process, allowing employers to submit postings for various opportunities. ICT staff can then efficiently review these postings and direct them to suitable candidates within the ICT student body.


Central Hub Functionality

This web-based solution provides a single point of contact for industry partners and ICT staff, facilitating effective communication and expediting the placement process. It's a tool that enhances connectivity and opportunities within the ICT community.
